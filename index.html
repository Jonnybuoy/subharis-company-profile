about us

We will provide you the best work which you dreamt for!

Founded in 2007, Subharis Kenya Limited ("Subharis") provides general contracting, construction management and pre-engineered metal building services to a broad range of residential, commercial and industrial clients throughout Kenya, including both private and government agencies. Anchored by loyal employees who are dedicated to providing superior service, and grounded in the philosophy that honesty and integrity are as important as the bottom line, Subharis has enjoyed an impeccable reputation for over a decade that it has been in operation. "Safety First" for all employees is embedded in our company culture from top to bottom.

building construction

house renovation

We specialize in house renovations, providing comprehensive solutions to transform your existing home into your dream space. From structural repairs to aesthetic upgrades, our experienced team ensures high-quality workmanship and attention to detail in every renovation project.

architechture design

We offer professional architectural design services, bringing creative and functional designs to life. Our team of skilled architects creates innovative and sustainable designs tailored to your specific needs, with a focus on aesthetics, functionality, and compliance with local building codes.

material supply

As a leading construction company in Kenya, we provide reliable and cost-effective material supply services for construction projects. We source and deliver high-quality construction materials, including cement, steel, timber, and more, ensuring timely and efficient delivery to your project site.

construction consultant

We offer expert construction consulting services to guide you through every step of your project. From project planning and budgeting to quality control and safety compliance, our experienced consultants provide invaluable insights and solutions to ensure the successful completion of your construction project.

interior design

We specialize in interior design services, creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces that reflect your personal style and requirements. Our team of interior designers offers creative solutions for residential and commercial spaces, from concept development to space planning, material selection, and installation.

building maintenance

We provide comprehensive building maintenance services to ensure the longevity and optimal performance of your property. From routine inspections to repairs and preventive maintenance, our skilled technicians and craftsmen offer reliable and efficient solutions to keep your building in top condition.

building renovation

We specialize in building renovation services, breathing new life into old or outdated structures. Our team of experienced professionals transforms existing buildings into modern and functional spaces, with a focus on quality, sustainability, and compliance with local building codes.
